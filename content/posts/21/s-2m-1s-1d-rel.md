A scratch note for system of two mass and one spring<!--more-->

$$\tag{1}
m_1 \frac{d^2 x_1}{dt^2} = k [ (x_2 - x_1) - l_0 ]
$$

and

$$\tag{2}
m_2 \frac{d^2 x_2}{dt^2} =  - k [ (x_2 - x_1) - l_0 ].
$$

Substract Eqn (2) with Eqn (1) to obtain following equations.
